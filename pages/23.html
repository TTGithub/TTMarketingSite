Real Estate Investment

ThruThinkSM is designed to meet the needs of Real Estate Investor. Each property can be entered as a Project along with it's income, expenses and the debt required to acquire the property. A full balance sheet, Income Statement and Cash Flow will be developed from the User Inputs. This can be a simple investment where either a portion of the Equity or 100% of the Equity is being purchased by the Investor or a more involved full Balance Sheet purchase of a real estate investment. There is a choice of five time horizons for the investment and an Exit Sale provision. There can be three classes of Equity ownership in a Project. Each class can have a different percentage of Equity and a different percentage of Gain on Exit Sale of the Company. The latter is designed to provide an Equity manager an incentive upon the Exit Sale of the Company. Multiple Projects can be saved and compared. The ThruThink Deal ScoreSM can be used as a comparison guage between Projects.

Sales, Cost of Goods Sold or alternatively EBITDA may be adjusted and managed along with Debt levels and repayment options and an array of Subordinated Debt options along with PIK Interest and Warrants. There is a Performance Earn Out provision for either the Seller or strategic investor.

The Individual Premium, Corporate Basic and Corporate Premium are subscriptions to use the ThruThinkSM site which will enable the User can make changes on up to 10 Projects for the Individual Premium and an unlimited* number of Projects on the Corporate Accounts.

ThruThinkSM Deal Score

Our ThruThink Deal ScoreSM service generates a score for the "Deal" using our propriatiary calculations for actual or proposed business transactions from user inputs for the purchase, sale, valuation, debt or equity analysis of a business. Our ThruThink Deal ScoreSM service is not necessarily a score for the quality of the Company itself, but a score for the "Deal". In other words, an excellent Company can receive a poor Deal Score because of the level of the purchase price or valuation, improper debt or equity structure, capital expenditures, owner draws, etc.

Our ThruThink Deal ScoreSM service is a rating of the "Deal" based on the user's inputs for a business's past performance, existing financial condition and projected future performance which will represent the performance of a particular purchase or value in concert with its unique debt and equity structure.

Level 2 — Investment Sector

The Investor would create a Summary Report for his or her investments and save it such as "Rebecca's Properties" or "Austin Area Properties" or "Bay Area Property Manager". The saved Summary Report would list specific key summary Data Points, listed for each of the Investor's investments and an average for each Data Point for all of the listed investments. The Investor would be the Author of this Report and give permission to others such as the Investors Bank or CPA. The Investor would have access to each of the Individual Projects that provided Permission to the Investor.

Level 3 — Total Company

The Investor would create a Summary Report of the all the Investors Area Summary Reports such as "Austin Area Properties", "Bay Area Properties", "Seatle Area Properties" and create a report for the Investors entire portfolio, i.e.. a summary of multiple summaries. For this report, the average for each area's Summary Data points will be listed under the Data Point and an average of all of the areas will be shown. The Investor will then save this as a Portfolio Report and provide Permission the Investor's Bank,or other Investors involved in the projects.
