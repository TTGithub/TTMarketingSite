Medium to Large Corporations

ThruThinkSM was designed to dive into the business performance, cash flow, debt and equity level management from a "big picture" point of view. Sales, Expense or more simply just EBITDA can be adjusted by percentages to quickly give a big picture look of overall performance, but can get down to detail by establishing each area, department, division, factory or location as a Project and with the Combination Function, bring them together in any number of summary combinations. At each level of combination, "big pitcure" adjustments can be made to the total.

ThruThinkSM provides the opportunity of inputting your summary Balance Sheet as the Existing Company and then purchasing a Target Company. The projections will then represent the combined operations of both companies with adjustments for the business performance of each entity to show the effect of an acquisition on the total performance of the combined company.

The Corporate Basic and Corporate Premium are subscriptions to use the ThruThinkSM site which will enable the User can make changes up an unlimited* number of Projects.

ThruThinkSM Deal Score

Our ThruThink Deal ScoreSM service generates a score for the "Deal" using our propriatiary calculations for actual or proposed business transactions from user inputs for the purchase, sale, valuation, debt or equity analysis of a business. Our ThruThink Deal ScoreSM service is not necessarily a score for the quality of the Company itself, but a score for the "Deal". In other words, an excellent Company can receive a poor Deal Score because of the level of the purchase price or valuation, improper debt or equity structure, capital expenditures, owner draws, etc.

Our ThruThink Deal ScoreSM service is a rating of the "Deal" based on the user's inputs for a business's past performance, existing financial condition and projected future performance which will represent the performance of a particular purchase or value in concert with its unique debt and equity structure.

Individual Budgets

For each Company, Data Inputs are inputted separately and saved as an Individual Project. Projects may be combined into one summary project. Individual Projects can be Companies, Products (sku's), Departments, Properties, Manufacturing sectors, Processing areas, Crops on a farm or any individual budgeted area. Whoever inputs the data will become the "Author". The Author can give access permission to specific individuals, for example, the Division Mgr. or VP of Mfg., the Senior VP of Operations and the CEO.

Level 2 — Divisions, Departments, Factories or Locations

The VP of Mfg. would execute a Combination Process to combine his or her Departments and save it such as "Alpha Mfg. Division". The saved Project would be a Combination Project and reflect the sum of all of the Departments in the Project. All input functionality is available in a Combination Project therefore Sales, Expenses, Debt, etc. can be fully adjusted, added or changed at the combination level. A Summary Report can also be created at this level. A Summary Report would list specific key summary Data Points, listed for each of the Departments and an average for each Data Point for all of the listed Departments. The VP of Mfg. will be the Author of both the Combination Project and the Summary Report and give permission to either or both to others such as the Senior VP and the CEO. The VP of Mfg. would have access to each of the Individual Department Projects that provided Permission to the VP of Mfg.

Level 3 — Total Company

The Senior VP would execute a Combination Process to combine his or her Divisions or Factories and save it such as "Beta Factory". The saved Project would be a Combination Project and reflect the sum of all of the Factories in the Project. All input functionality is available in a Combination Project therefore Sales, Expenses, Debt, etc. can be fully adjusted, added or changed at this combination level. A Summary Report can also be created at this level. A Summary Report would list specific key summary Data Points, listed for each of the Factories and an average for each Data Point for all of the listed Factories. The Senior VP will be the Author of both the Combination Project and the Summary Report and give permission to either or both to others such as the CEO. The Senior VP and CEO would have access to each of the Individual Department Projects and Division Projects and Summary Reports that provided Permission to the Senior VP and CEO.
