Advisory / CPA Firm

ThruThinkSM was born out of the realization that businesses are started and run with passion, but survive and grow with numbers. Some people just don't deal with numbers well and therefore suffer the consequenses of failure or are able to survive to the point of being able to hire good "numbers people". These are your Clients; the Individuals with an established business, entrepreneurs planning out a new business, retirees planning a new chapter in their lives or simply individuals with a dream. Only a small percentage of these individuals will possess the vision and passion together with the affinity for numbers that are required for long term business success. ThruThinkSM is designed to bridge this gap.

Advisors and CPA's are often asked by their clients: Is this a good Deal, Is this too much Debt, How am I doing? Your Clients expect a quick and simple answer to an often complicated question. In order to answer these questions, the Advisor or CPA either must give a very generalized answer or must spend a great deal of time to dig into the Deal. ThruThinkSM is intended to provide the Advisor or CPA a time saving alternative that will give the "numbers" perspective to their Clients that will help them answer their questions.

Data Inputs are inputted and saved as an Individual Project. Each Project can represent different "scenarios" representing different risk levels or opportunities. Whoever inputs the data will become the "Author". The Author can give access permission to Corporate Accounts, for example the client, banker, investor or other advisor to access the Project.

The Individual Premium, Corporate Basic and Corporate Premium are subscriptions to use the ThruThinkSM site which will enable the User can make changes on up to 10 Projects for the Individual Premium and an unlimited* number of Projects on the Corporate Accounts.

ThruThinkSM Deal Score

Our ThruThink Deal ScoreSM service generates a score for the "Deal" using our propriatiary calculations for actual or proposed business transactions from user inputs for the purchase, sale, valuation, debt or equity analysis of a business. Our ThruThink Deal ScoreSM service is not necessarily a score for the quality of the Company itself, but a score for the "Deal". In other words, an excellent Company can receive a poor Deal Score because of the level of the purchase price or valuation, improper debt or equity structure, capital expenditures, owner draws, etc.

Our ThruThink Deal ScoreSM service is a rating of the "Deal" based on the user's inputs for a business's past performance, existing financial condition and projected future performance which will represent the performance of a particular purchase or value in concert with its unique debt and equity structure.

Level 2 — Company Summary by Advisor

The Advisor would create a Summary Report for his or her Company Projects and save it such as "Rebecca's Companies". The saved Summary Report would list specific key summary Data Points, listed for each of the Advisors Company Projects and an average for each Data Point for all of the listed Companies. The Advisor would be the Author of this Report and give permission to others such as the Senior Partner and/or the CEO. The Advisor would have access to each of the Individual Projects that provided Permission to the Advisor.

Level 3 — Portfolio or Client Summary

A Senior Partner would create a Summary Report of the all the Advisors Summary Reports, i.e.. a summary of multiple summaries. For this report, for each Advisor, the average for each of the Summary Data points by Advisor will be listed under the Data Point and an average of all of the Advisors will be shown. The Senior Partner will then save this as a Summary Report and provide Permission to the Managing Partner or CEO. The CEO can then prepare another Summary Report showing the averages by Senior Partner that represents the whole firm and if desired can in turn give Permissions to the Report back through the management chain. The CEO and Managing Partner would have access to each Individual Project and the Advisors Summary Reports as long as the Permissions for each have included those individuals.
