With the ThruThinkSM corporate account, individual Projects can be combined into one summary Project using the Combine Multiple Project feature. The summary Project can be managed just like an individual Project whereby income, expenses, and debt may be added to the Combined Projects summary totals and a new Combined Project saved.

With the premium corporate account, this can be used with multiple divisions or factories of one company, whereby each division is a separate Project and the Combined Project adds the corporate overhead expense and debt for the entire company or similarly with multiple locations of one company or in the case of a franchise, multiple franchise locations combined together to represent the entire franchise operation.
