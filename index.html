What is ThruThinkSM

ThruThinkSM is an interactive business simulation model that illustrates the projected performance of a business in concert with its debt and equity structure.

ThruThinkSM is used for the analysis, capital restructure or purchase of an existing company, target company, factory expansion, real estate deal or an entrepreneur's dream.
